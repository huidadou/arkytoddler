No paywall, no tiers, no plan to scale. Just an invitation, if reading has been worth something to you.

Arkytoddler is a one-person operation, written in the cracks of a working life. There's no paywall, no membership funnel, no plan to "scale." It exists because I find the writing more interesting than not writing it — and because a few of you keep reading, which is the part that surprises me most.

If something here has been useful, or odd in a way you liked, or worth a quiet huh on the train home — you can throw a few dollars in the jar. It mostly covers hosting, the occasional book I buy to think with, and the general project of keeping this thing free and ad-free.

Reading and occasionally telling a friend is also a form of support, and an easier one. No obligation either way.
